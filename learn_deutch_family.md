While I can certainly help you understand the German 
language, learning a new language requires consistent 
practice and immersion. Here are some basic German phrases to 
get started:

1. Guten Tag - Good day
2. Hallo - Hello
3. Danke - Thank you
4. Bitte - Please
5. Entschuldigung - Excuse me/I'm sorry
6. Ja - Yes
7. Nein - No
8. Viel Zeit - Lots of time
9. Wie geht es dir? - How are you? (informal)
10. Wie geht's? - How do you do? (formal)
11. Ich verstehe nicht - I don't understand
12. Sprechen Sie Englisch? - Do you speak English?
13. Wo ist die Toilette? - Where is the bathroom?
14. Kann ich...? - Can I...? (used for polite requests)
15. Bitte schön - You're welcome

For learning German, consider using language learning apps 
like Duolingo, Babbel, or Rosetta Stone, watching German 
movies or TV shows with subtitles, reading books, and 
practicing speaking with native speakers if possible.
